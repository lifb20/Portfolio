Glad you made it! Here's a bit about me –

I am currently a final year student at Imperial College London, pursuing a Master of Engineering in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (with management). My passion lies in anything and everything technology, particularly digital system design, machine learning and full-stack development – feel free to browse some of my past projects on the next page or peek my CV for details!

I believe the key behind any successful team or group project is empathy, enthusiasm and open-communication. These are principles I have applied to every group project of mine, as well as during my time as duty-manager, where I had the honour of leading a bar team of talented individuals during events with over 800 attendees. Furthermore, growing up in a multilingual household and environment has continuously allows me to thrive in the most diverse settings.

When it comes to challenges, I believe the most interesting ones are those with no clear solution and which require a creative approach. That being said, to solve a problem optimally, one should have a good understanding of the underlying theory, in addition to hands-on experience, to fully appreciate the ins-and-outs of the challenge at hand. This is also my approach to learning, where I aim for a solid understanding of the theory, as well as sufficient practical experience.

Beyond academia and technical interests, I enjoy travelling, going to the gym and running – be it short or long distance! In fact, I am proud to have completed my first-ever half-marathon last summer with my girlfriend and look forward to running a full one in the not-too-distant future!
